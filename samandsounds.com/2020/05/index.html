Addicted To You

Addicted To You – Sam and Sounds

‘It’s a fun, energetic, and suspenseful 3 minutes of your time that you won’t regret spending’. If you heard this from someone you may think they’re talking about a rollercoaster, or the last few minutes of the World Cup Final – but here, we’re talking about Sam and Sounds’ newest release, ‘Addicted To You’. After gaining a listener-base from his debut single, that far exceeded anyone’s expectations, he has followed up with what is arguably a stronger track for his 2nd release.

Sam speaks on the track below –

‘This song was written on a 45 minute train journey home from London, when I was feeling particularly grateful. I had been suffering for several years with shocking mental health conditions and had seemingly been pushed to the breaking point multiple times. One particular Sunday, about 5/6 months before I wrote this song, I was at church and during the worship part of the service, I heard what I believe to be God’s voice saying the words – ‘that’s it, you’re coming out of it’ – over and over again in my head. Literally, from that point, I no longer suffered from the unexplained anxiety and depression that had virtually ruled my life for the best part of 2 years. The lyrics are a direct message to Jesus, celebrating who he is and what he did for me!’

The song is out on the 15th of May and you’ll be able to listen to it on major streaming platforms!
