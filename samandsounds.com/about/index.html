HEY!

Hey! Sam and Sounds here! I’m a 19 year old indie pop artist originally from London, but currently living in leafy Surrey. I’ve been writing songs since the age of 11 and dreaming about becoming an artist for as long as I can remember – and at age 17 I had a realisation that I had to pursue music with all I had! I started busking in 2019 in order to train my voice up to the standard it needed to be, whilst earning enough money to be able to quit the physical labour job I had at the time. Busking around the towns and cities of the south-east part of England started to generate a small following, to which I have just released my debut single, ‘Do It All Again’.

I have many more songs to come, and it is my pure passion to write music, so be sure to stay tuned to hear the bangers that are on their way!
